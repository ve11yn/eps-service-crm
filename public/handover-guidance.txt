Handover guidance for Gage's CRM

Ownership
- Gage owns the domain, hosting, Supabase project, WhatsApp Business API account, and any AI or accounting integrations.

Roles
- Owner: full operational control, final approvals, access to all data and settings.
- Admin: manage users, review drafts, approve quotes, assign work, reconcile jobs, and oversee closeout.
- Coordinator: manage day-to-day scheduling, intake, and customer communication.
- Field worker: receive assigned work, update job status, and upload evidence for completed work.

What users can do
- Login with their assigned role only.
- Review WhatsApp intake, projects, schedules, and assigned work.
- Create or edit work items through the review workflow.
- Upload photos and supporting evidence where permitted.
- View reports and handover notes if they have the right access.

Operational notes
- Keep API keys, webhook secrets, and service-role credentials with the owner.
- Rotate access when staff changes or vendors are replaced.
- Apply Supabase migrations before validating auth and data routes.
- Keep exported copies of quotes, invoices, and evidence files outside the app as part of the owner handover.
