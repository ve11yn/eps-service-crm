# Training Plan

## Admin workshop — 2 hours

- Customer/property setup and duplicate merging
- Lead qualification, catalogue pricing, quote revisions and approvals
- Scheduling, team assignment, issue resolution and QA
- Invoices, payments, reports and audit review
- Practice: run one sample enquiry from customer creation through invoice

## Worker workshop — 45 minutes

- Find an assigned job, record travel/arrival/start
- Upload before/during/after evidence
- Report each structured issue type
- Complete a checklist and recover from poor reception

## Owner workshop — 60 minutes

- Decision queue, discount/add-on approval, audit and performance reports
- QA exceptions, migration validation and final shutdown sign-off

Record attendance, questions and retraining actions in the UAT/rollout workspace.
