# Owner Approval SOP

Owner approval is required for discounts outside policy, disputes, complex scope changes, add-ons, QA exceptions and Notion shutdown.

1. Read the current scope, decision-needed summary, quote/project history and original evidence.
2. Record the decision in the CRM; do not approve only in an external chat.
3. For discounts, confirm the original total, discount value, reason and resulting margin.
4. For add-ons/rework, confirm responsibility, price change, schedule impact and customer agreement.
5. For QA, check evidence, customer sign-off and unresolved alerts.
6. For Notion shutdown, require a clean validation report, completed parity checklist, resolved parallel-run exceptions and named sign-off.
